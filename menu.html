Menu Overview

The menu at Whatever You Want offers a captivating fusion of Mexican and Asian cuisines, with an emphasis on vibrant flavors and unique culinary influences. Signature dishes like the Hibachi Burrito showcase innovative cooking techniques that blend traditional methods with modern flair. This diverse menu is thoughtfully designed around seasonal ingredients sourced from local farmers to ensure freshness in every bite. Specialties such as cream cheese rangoons exemplify the restaurant's commitment to creative comfort food, making it a delightful dining experience for all patrons seeking something extraordinary.

Dietary Options

Whatever You Want prioritizes inclusivity by providing a variety of dietary options tailored to different preferences and restrictions. The menu features delicious vegetarian and vegan selections alongside gluten-free choices that cater to health-conscious diners without compromising flavor or satisfaction. Allergen awareness is paramount; staff members are trained to assist guests with specific needs, ensuring meals can be customized effectively for individual requirements. Additionally, special dietary menus are available upon request, further enhancing the accessibility of this unique dining destination.
